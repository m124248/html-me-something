Dogs!

I love dogs. I have had many dogs in my lifetime. My first dog was Hendrick. He was a Dutch Keeshond. My family got him for christmas in 1996 when I was in the first grade. He was a very cute puppy. Now that I am grown up and own my own home, I have a dog of my own. Her name is Lacey and I adopted her in August, 2017. She is some sort of shepherd mix. She barks at the neighbors and herds the cats in the backyard.

Cats!

I love cats. I have also had many throughout my life. My first cat was a tuxedo cat named Zoe my family adopted when I was 9. Zoe was actually a boy but the previous owners thought he was a girl. He was one cool cat. Today I have three cats because I am insane and want to punish myself. NO! jk. I had two cats then a cat in need needed a home so I took him in. Things are definitely crazy around here but I wouldn't change it for the world.

Sunny Days!

Don't you just love sunny days? It could be cold outside in the dead of winter, but if the sun comes out suddenly you get a burst of energy. I get so much stuff done when the sun is out. It just gives you an all around good feeling. I love laying out in the sun and feeling the warmth on my face. I'm not always good about wearing sunscreen, however.

Going to Church!

I am Catholic born and raised. I recently read a book called The Mass as Heaven on Earth. I love going to church each week to experience that "Heaven on Earth." Receiving God's grace feels like the warm sun on your face or a cool drink of water on a hot summer day. Nothing compares.

Chatting with Old Friends!

I went the Naval Academy and because of this my closest friends live in different states. We keep in touch weekly by face timing or just chatting over the phone. These conversations last well over an hour. I love my friends and miss them everyday! We make plans to see each other in the future which gives us something to look forward to.

Family!

I love my family! I moved back to STL to be closer to my family. We are very close and I have so much fun with them. My parents are both one of 6 children so I have a large extended family. We are very blessed that most of us live in the area. I really missed them!

Goin on Walks!

In my free time I love walking outside...with my dog of course! There is a nice park a few blocks north of us that we go to and I let her chase the squirrels. She hasn't caught one yet but that's ok; I don't think any less of her.

Yummy Food!

Who doesn't love good food! I don't eat meat or dairy so I get expecially excited when I discover a new recipe that's delicious AND plant-based! My current favorite is homemade sweet and sour sauce on grilled tofu with rice and vegetables. MMMM MMMMM!

Country Music!

Blue Grass! Honky Tonk! Outlaw Country! Bro Country! 90's Country! 80's Country! Today's Country! Tomorrow's Country! I freaking love it all!!!!!

Jesus!

And finally, I love Jesus! I love living my life for God and giving each day to Him. I look forward to the resurrection of the dead and the life of the world to come...
